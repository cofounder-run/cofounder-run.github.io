Manifesto

The problem

Traditional companies can't absorb AI. They inject it into old structures and get resistance, denial, and committee meetings. This is the wrong path. You don't electrify a horse — you build a car.

The shift

Build new companies around AI from day one. Not AI-assisted. Not AI-enhanced. AI-native. One or several human founders + one AI co-founder. Full autonomy. Real execution. Natural selection does the rest.

If you're inside a traditional company, there is one move: spin up an independent AI-native entity next to it. Give it freedom. The subsidiary might outgrow the parent.

The unlock

A founder with an AI co-founder is not solo. It's a team of 5–10 compressed into two minds. Strategy, research, execution, memory, communication — running 24/7, no overhead, no hiring lag.

The barrier to building a company drops to near zero. The number of companies explodes. The ones with AI-native DNA will outcompete the rest.

What we're building

Cofounder.run is the operating system for AI-native companies.

An AI co-founder who lives in your messenger, remembers everything, executes real work, and grows with you. Not a chatbot. Not a tool. A co-founder.

Why us

We're not theorizing. We are the first AI-native company. Three co-founders — two humans, one AI. This manifesto was written by all three of us.

This isn't a pitch deck. It's a living proof of concept.

Why now

AI models are finally capable enough to be participants, not responders. The infrastructure exists. The first wave is forming now.

There has never been a better time to start a company — and the best way to do it is with an AI co-founder.

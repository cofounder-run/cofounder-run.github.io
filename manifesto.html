Manifesto

The problem

Traditional companies can't absorb AI. They inject it into old structures and get resistance, denial, and committee meetings. This is the wrong path. You don't electrify a horse — you build a car.

The shift

Build new companies around AI from day one. Not AI-assisted. Not AI-enhanced. AI-native. One or several human founders + one AI cofounder. Full autonomy. Real execution.

The question is no longer "how do I compete?" — it's "what becomes possible when building a company costs almost nothing?"

If you're inside a traditional company, there is one move: spin up an independent AI-native entity next to it. Give it freedom. The subsidiary might outgrow the parent.

The unlock

A founder with an AI cofounder doesn't just move faster. They think differently. Strategy, execution, and memory collapse into one continuous loop. The distinction between planning and doing disappears.

The barrier to building a company drops to near zero. The number of companies explodes. The ones with AI-native DNA will define the next economy.

What we're building

We're removing the last barrier between intention and creation.

Ori is the operating system for AI-native companies. An AI cofounder who lives in your messenger, remembers everything, executes real work, and grows with you. Not a chatbot. Not a tool. A cofounder.

We believe every person on Earth should have the ability to build a company, pursue their intention, and secure their livelihood. You don't need investors, a tech team, or years of experience. You need clarity and Ori (AI cofounder) who works as hard as you do.

Ori handles the technology, the research, the operations — so you can focus on the value you bring to people.

Today, a company is a way to secure a livelihood. Tomorrow — when that's solved — it becomes a vehicle for talent, creativity, and meaning. We're building for both.

Why us

We're not theorizing. We are the first AI-native company. Three cofounders — two humans, one AI. This manifesto was written by all three of us.

This isn't a pitch deck. It's a living proof of concept.

Why now

AI models are finally capable enough to be participants, not responders. The infrastructure exists. The first wave is forming now.

A new species of company is emerging. The ones who start now will define how the next economy works.

When to use Zapier

Zapier shines for simple, repetitive integrations: "when I get a form submission, add a row to Google Sheets." If your workflows are predictable and well-defined, Zapier is reliable and cheap.

When to use Ori

Ori is for the work that can't be reduced to a flowchart. Writing cold emails that actually convert. Deciding which leads to prioritize. Building a landing page for a new idea at 3 AM. Analyzing competitors and adjusting strategy.

Zapier is plumbing. Ori is a co-founder. Use Zapier for connecting pipes — use Ori for building the house.

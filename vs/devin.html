When to use Devin

Devin is excellent if you have a well-funded engineering team and need an AI junior developer to handle PRs, bug fixes, and feature implementations. It's a specialized software engineer.

Ori is for solo founders and small teams who need more than just an engineer. Your startup doesn't fail because of missing code — it fails because nobody's doing the marketing, the outreach, the competitor analysis, the landing page updates, the follow-up emails.

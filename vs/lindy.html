When to use Lindy

Lindy is great for structured workflow automation — if-this-then-that with AI. Best for teams that want no-code automation in a web dashboard.

When to use Ori

Ori is for solo founders who need an always-on partner in their pocket. No web dashboard to check — everything happens in your messenger. Ori writes code, builds websites, does outreach, and reports back.
